Philippine Trigon Shipyard Corp.

A fully integrated shipyard facility capable of performing extensive repair works, conversion, modification and ship building using parts re-cycled from the ship breaking activities. Its cargo handling facility allows out to be very flexible in its shipyard activities because it can at the same time load or discharge ships while being repaired in its facility.

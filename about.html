About Us

  1. Home
  2. About

The yard site of Philippine Trigon Shipyard Corporation is located in a 20-hectares protective-cove area located in Sangat, San Fernando, Cebu. It is about 40-km south of the port of Cebu City, at 123°E 41' 28.31" longitude and 10°N 7' 40.99" latitude.

The shipyard is supplied with a reliable shore power supply from the CEBECO 2 electric power transmission. The yard has full access to the supply chain needed in the on-time delivery of materials, machinery parts and essential dry-docking items delivered either by air, sea or land.

The 500-skilled workers working in the yard are majority indigenous to the towns nearby to the location of the shipyard. Specialized technical skills in the repair of ship machineries, if needed at an instant can readily be tapped from several sub-contractors which Trigon has established a significant network. Management of the shipyard remained with immediate family members and is professionally run by already by the third generation.

Mission

Our shipyard is committed to providing cost effective and technically sound services for shipbuilding, ship repair and other auxiliary marine repair works for ocean-going, water-borne structures of sized up to 7,000 deadweight capacity in support of the national government’s effort to provide seaworthy and economically sustainable sea transport network to the country.

Quality

The shipyard is committed to satisfy the needs and expectations of our customers by providing a balanced combination of scare capital resources, equipment, machinery and tools with human experience and expertise in planning, designing and management to ensure effective implementation of cost-effective services without compromising quality.

Human Resource

In as much as people are said to be the most important commodity in any organization, we are committed to the development of their abilities and train them in the areas of technical upgrading, planning, and organizing, leading and controlling while making their way of life challenging, rewarding, and uplifting to their spirits.

Customer

Whatever the requirements, from shipbuilding to ship repair, from propulsion/machinery repairs to electronics and vessel deck equipment, the shipyard stands for the symbol of readiness – all with safety and awareness for whatever our valued clients require.

Environment

We strive to be environmentally friendly, abiding wholeheartedly with the intent and purposes of the environmental regulations of the government.

Ethics

To conform with our policy of accommodating out customers but not compromising our being truthful, honest, sincere, and God fearing; and to follow the principle of society first, profession second, and self last.
